PROJECT CLUB (SYNC)

As the name suggests, here seniors and freshers of TSEC work in 'Sync' to turn their imagination into reality. This club gives budding engineers an opportunity to play with electronic components and do wonders. Under the guidance of seniors, freshers get a chance to try their hands at robotics and software applications in their first year itself. These projects are displayed in our festivals where they are judged and the best project is awarded. All the participants receive a Letter Of Appreciation for their efforts. In this way, Sync helps nurture the creative technical minds of our college.
